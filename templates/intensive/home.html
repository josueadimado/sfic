WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

During the 3-Day Spiritual Warfare Intensive, participants will enter a focused journey of biblical learning, spiritual renewal, and practical ministry training. This experience is designed to equip believers with a deeper understanding of spiritual conflict and the freedom Christ provides.

BIBLICAL TEACHING

Receive clear, Scripture-centered teaching on spiritual warfare, spiritual freedom, and the authority believers share with Christ.

PRACTICAL MINISTRY TRAINING

Learn biblical principles and practical tools that can be applied in personal life, discipleship, and responsible ministry to others.

FREEDOM AND HEALING

Many participants begin experiencing spiritual breakthrough, healing, and freedom from long-standing spiritual struggles and bondage.

A COMMUNITY OF GROWTH

Connect with believers who are pursuing spiritual maturity, freedom in Christ, and deeper transformation together.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

During the 3-Day Spiritual Warfare Intensive, participants will engage in Scripture-based teaching and guided learning designed to build a clear understanding of spiritual warfare and the freedom found in Christ.

Participants will explore:

  • The biblical foundations of spiritual warfare and the great controversy between Christ and the powers of darkness
  • The authority believers receive through Christ and how to live in that authority
  • How spiritual bondage develops and the common gateways that allow spiritual oppression
  • Biblical pathways to freedom, healing, and lasting spiritual restoration

Over the course of three focused days, this intensive training equips believers with biblical knowledge, spiritual discernment, and practical ministry principles to walk confidently in Christ's freedom and to support others seeking restoration.
